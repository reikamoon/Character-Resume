>Biography<

CV: Kaito Ishikawa/Chris Hackney

My name is Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd, and I am the crown prince of the Holy Kingdom of Faerghus.
I am the son of the late king, King Lambert Egitte Blaiddyd, and his consort. I was born on the 20th of the Ethereal Moon (December 20) during the Imperial
year 1162. I am currently 18 years of age, and as I am still too young to ascend the throne in my father's place, my paternal uncle, Rufus, acts as regent.
I attend the Officer's Academy at Garreg Mach Monastery, and am the current house leader of the Blue Lions, which is skillfully taught by my beloved
professor. I excel in combat, mostly utilizing lances and swords. Due to my bearing of the crest Blaiddyd, I was born with immense strength.
